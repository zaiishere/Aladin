Tohru Honda

About Tohru

Tohru Honda is the portagonist of Fruits Basket, a slice of life, romance and comdedy anime. Always the bright and cheerful girl, Tohru does her best to make the best of everything. She's my comfort character as her constant battle with self-guilt and her kindness is somnehow relatable. Tohru goes too hard on herself and eventually learns it's okay to be "selfish" and love someone

Tohru's Characterstics

  1. Tohru is an orphan who lived in a tent for a while
  2. Her personality trait is ESFP
  3. Blames herself for her mother's death.
  4. Very lonely even though surrounded by friends she loves
  5. Tohru feels like she must love her mother the most. She is afraid of forgetting her and is griefing heavily
  6. Finally accepts she loves kyo too which she thinks is selfish of her

Tohru's Friends

Tohru considers everyone her friends but she has two best friends:Uotani and Hanajima. The three girls have a deep bond. Tohru even considers them her family. Her other friends are Yuki, Momji, Kyo, Rin and many more.
Tohru ends up with the tsundere ginger cat, kyo and I guarrentee you'll swoon over them if you watch the series
